# Examples

## SmFeO₃ synthesis time series

The repository ships a real worked example: a powder-XRD **time series** of an
SmFeO₃ synthesis, measured at 1, 5, 10, 12, 14, 16, 18, 20, 22 and 24 hours, together
with reference structures.

**The scientific question:** as the synthesis progresses (1 h → 24 h), do the
precursor / secondary phases disappear and the SmFeO₃ phase form?

**Reference structures provided**

- `SmFeO₃` — the target phase
- `Sm₂O₃` — a possible secondary phase
- metallic `Fe` — a possible precursor

The data and CIFs live in the
[`examples/` folder](https://github.com/SrValentim/MatFinder/tree/main/examples)
of the repository, with a step-by-step walkthrough in its
[README](https://github.com/SrValentim/MatFinder/blob/main/examples/README.md).
