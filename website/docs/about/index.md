# The project

**MatFinder** is developed at the **Federal University of Amazonas (UFAM)**, Brazil,
in the Department of Physics (Materials Physics). It is free and open-source software,
released under the GPL-3.0 license.

## Mission

To give students and laboratories — especially those without access to commercial
software — an **integrated, open and accessible** tool for the everyday powder-XRD
workflow: from searching open databases to identifying phases.

## Status

MatFinder is **actively developed and maintained**. See the
[CHANGELOG](https://github.com/SrValentim/MatFinder/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md) for the
full version history.

Recent highlights:

- PhaseDRX as a standalone app with cross-app CIF export
- Full UI translation (Portuguese, English, German)
- Open-access article retrieval by DOI (OpenAlex / Unpaywall)
- Runs from source on Windows, Linux and macOS

## Acknowledgments

MatFinder relies on the open databases it integrates — the **Materials Project**,
**COD**, **OQMD** and **ROD** — and on the **pymatgen** project, as well as the wider
Python scientific ecosystem (PySide6, NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib).
